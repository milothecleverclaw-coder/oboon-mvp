## Key Assumptions

- Operating hours: 9am-4pm, 6 hours/day
- Call frequency: 2 meetings/month per prisoner, 20 min each
- Peak concurrent calls: ~100
- B200 GPU: ~฿2M each
- Thai senior engineer: ฿80k-120k/month
- Year 1 team = founders (equity) + contractors

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Year 1 delivery fails | Milestone gates, cloud flexibility |
| Can't hire engineers | Competitive salaries, remote options |
| GPU prices spike | Buy in Year 2, have budget buffer |
| ISO27001 delayed | Start early, hire consultant |
| Spending overrun | 74% margin provides buffer |
