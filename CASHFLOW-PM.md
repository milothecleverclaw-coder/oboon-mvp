# Oboon Prison Video Call System - Deployment & Funding Plan

> **Scope:** 6 prisons, 10,000 prisoners  
> **Contract Duration:** 5 years  
> **Total Funding Request:** ฿195,000,000 (Year 1)  
> **Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## Executive Summary

We are requesting **฿195,000,000** in Year 1 to deploy a prison video call system across 6 prisons serving 10,000 prisoners. 

**Strategy:**
- **Year 1:** Lean deployment using cloud infrastructure. Extremely cost-efficient, demonstrates ability to deliver and generate returns.
- **Years 2-5:** Heavy investment in Thai sovereignty, compliance (ISO27001, PDPA), and building a world-class Thai engineering team.

| Metric | Year 1 | Years 2-5 |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Deployment Spend | ~฿35M | ~฿160M |
| Infrastructure | Cloud (rented) | Owned (Thai datacenter) |
| Team | 3-5 (contractors) | 15-20 (full-time) |
| Compliance | Basic | ISO27001 + PDPA |
| GPU | Cloud (Modal/Runpod) | Owned (B200 in Thailand) |

---

## Why This Structure Attracts Investors

| Concern | Our Answer |
|---------|------------|
| "Can they deliver?" | Year 1 proves deployment capability with minimal spend |
| "Can they repay?" | Year 1 spend is only ฿35M of ฿195M - massive margin for loan repayment |
| "Is it sustainable?" | Years 2-5 investment ensures long-term Thai sovereignty & compliance |
| "What about data laws?" | All data moves to Thai soil after Year 1 |

## Years 2-5: Thai Sovereignty Investment (~฿160M)

### Why Invest After Year 1?

1. **Thai Data Sovereignty** - PDPA requires sensitive data (prisoner video calls) to stay in Thailand
2. **ISO27001 Compliance** - Enterprise/government contracts require certification
3. **Cost Efficiency** - Owning GPUs cheaper than cloud long-term
4. **Team Building** - Full-time Thai engineers for long-term maintenance
5. **Government Trust** - Thai-owned infrastructure builds trust with Ministry of Justice
