> **Scope:** 6 prisons, 10,000 prisoners

We are requesting **£5,000,000** to deploy a prison video call system across 6 prisons serving 10,000 prisoners over 5 years. This document outlines the funding milestones, budget allocation, and success criteria for Year 1.

## Key Assumptions

- Operating hours: 9am-4pm, 6 hours/day
- Call frequency: 2 meetings/month per prisoner, 20 min each
- Peak concurrent calls: ~100
- 50,000 family accounts expected
- Video traffic: Domestic (Thailand) only
- AI processing: International cloud required
- Exchange rate: 1 GBP ≈ 43 THB
